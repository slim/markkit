Add the markkit button to your site

markkit is a web2.0 text highlighter. Copy/Paste the code in suitable location for the markkit button to appear in your site. Your visitors will be able to highlight by clicking the markkit button. markkit is in early stages of developement, it works only with Mozilla Firefox and Apple Safari (but it does not break other browsers).
